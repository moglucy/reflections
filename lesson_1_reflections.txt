cd - change directory

cd Documents (change to Documents directory)

cd .. (go back up a directory)

cd ~ (change to your home directory)

ls (list the contents of the directory)

ls -f (shows folders/ then files - folders are followed by a slash)

la -a (shows hidden files and folders)

la -af (both the above)

pwd - shows where you are

mkdir - make directory (make a folder)

\ use a backspace where there is a space in name ie my games = my\ games

echo 'alias npd="C:Windows/SysWOW64/notepad.exe"'>>~/bashrc (sets npd as a quick link to notepad)
